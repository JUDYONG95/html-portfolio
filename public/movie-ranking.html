Judy's Top Movie Recommendations

My top 3 movies of all time

Shawshank Redemption

Despite being a relatively old film, the context and main messaging of the film remain relevant. It is a hopeful film and I highly recommend this.

3 Idiots

Another film with a meaningful message to stay hopeful and challenge the societal norms of pursuing academic success.

Guardians of the Galaxy Vol. 3

One of the latest films I watched this year, yet it remains memorable because of the friendship it portrays. Plus points for managing to incorporate both comedic and touching elements in the show.
